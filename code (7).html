Our Story

Since 1985, Fred's Furnitures has been crafting heirloom-quality furniture in California. What began as a small workshop in Santa Barbara has grown into a beloved brand known for its attention to detail and commitment to sustainable materials.

Each piece in our collection is handcrafted by our skilled artisans using techniques passed down through generations. We source our wood from responsibly managed forests and take pride in creating furniture that stands the test of time.
